Terms of Service

Registration and Payment

You must register online at www.code-prep.com. Full payment is due at the time of registration. Your child will not be enrolled in any camp or series of classes until a completed registration form and full payment are received.

Changes and Refunds

If a camp or class series must be canceled by CodePrep due to lack of enrollement, a full refund will be provided. Camp withdrawals prior to June 1 must be received by email sent to registration@code-prep.com and are assessed a $25 processing fee. There are no refunds for camp withdrawals after June 1. A $25 processing fee will be assessed for camp or class changes. CodePrep does not prorate the cost of any programs.

Code of Conduct

Attendees of CodePrep camps or classes are expected to behave with respect to the instructors, their fellow students, and the physical property of CodePrep and its students. Students are expected to show care for the camp or class venue. They are also expected to participate in all camp and class activities and refrain from browsing unrelated material on the internet, partaking in unrelated computer work, or using cell phones without permission from the instructor.

If there is a situation regarding a student's misbehavior, the instructor will take the student aside and remind him or her of CodePrep's Code of Conduct. If the problem persists, the CodePrep founder will speak directly to the student in order to resolve the problem and will also call the student's parent or guardian. If the problem continues after speaking with the parent or guardian, the child will be removed from the program and no refund will be given.
